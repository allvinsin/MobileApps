Descriptions

Allvins takes your privacy seriously. With the exception of the cases listed below or in the applicable End User License Agreements, our software does not transmit any information over the Internet that could be used to identify the person or device that is using the software.

Advertising

The software products provided by Allvins may display advertising which is obtained from third party advertising networks.

1. Where you are using the software products;
2. What kind of device you are using the software products on;

No personally identifiable data is ever transmitted. While certain advertising networks permit the software products to transmit demographic information such as age, gender etc, such information will never be shared by this software products.
